Biography

I am Gou Tan (谭苟 in Chinese). I am a 2th year MPhil at Sun Yat-Sen University, advised by Professor Pengfei Chen. I am interested in Cloud Computing, AI Reliability, AIOps, and Microservice. My research focus on the observability of AI model performance in cloud native environments. And I have a strong interest in large language models (LLM).

I have applied for a combined Master’s and Ph.D. program, and I expect to become a first-year Ph.D. student in September 2024.
